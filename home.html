Our Story

CLASSIC RECIPES BAKED FRESH DAILY

Amer Bakery Hut opened its doors in 1994 as the First Gourmet Bakery of Bhopal. We introduced the concept of fast food dining in Bhopal. Its been 27 glorious years and we are privileged to serve people from all walks of life. Be it your child's first birthday party, a family get together, kitty party, a nervous first date, a college bunk or just plain book reading, we have always been there for you. This remarkable journey was only possible because of your support, encouragement, and feedback.
